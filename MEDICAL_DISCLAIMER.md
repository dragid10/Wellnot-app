## Not Medical Advice

The information you record in this app is for your own personal reference only. Nothing in this app should be interpreted as medical advice, a clinical recommendation, or a substitute for professional medical care.

## Consult a Healthcare Professional

If you have concerns about your health, are experiencing symptoms, or need medical advice, please consult a qualified healthcare professional. Do not delay seeking medical attention based on anything you have recorded or observed in this app.

## Accuracy

This app records information exactly as you enter it. It does not validate, interpret, or analyze your health data. The accuracy and completeness of the information depends entirely on your own input.
