# Privacy Policy

**Wellnot**
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026
**Developer:** Alex Oladele

## Overview

Wellnot is a mobile application for tracking daily symptoms, moods, and health patterns. The app is designed to be fully offline and local-first. Your data belongs to you and stays on your device.

## Data Collection

Wellnot does **not** collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data with the developer, third parties, or external servers. The app has no backend, no analytics, no advertising, and no network calls.

All data you enter — including symptom entries, moods, tags, notes, and custom symptom/tag definitions — is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device.

Notification preferences (reminder time, toggle states) are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.

## Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with anyone. There are no third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud services integrated into this app.

## Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. The data is protected by your device's built-in security features (screen lock, encryption). The developer has no access to your data.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all of your data at any time from within the app using the "Clear All Data" option in the settings screen. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data from your device.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions, all of which are optional and only prompted when you enable the relevant feature:

| Permission | Purpose | When Requested |
|---|---|---|
| Notifications | Send daily reminder notifications | When you enable the Daily Reminder toggle |
| Exact Alarms | Fire reminders at your chosen time | When you enable the Daily Reminder toggle |

No permissions are requested at app startup. You can revoke any permission at any time through your device's system settings.

## Health Data

Wellnot allows you to log symptoms, moods, and tags for personal tracking purposes. This app is **not a medical device** and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. The information recorded in this app should not be considered medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical guidance.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since no data is collected or transmitted at all, there is no risk of inadvertent collection of children's data.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in a new version of the app and the effective date above will be updated.
